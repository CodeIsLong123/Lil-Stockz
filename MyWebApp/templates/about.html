About us

This app is supposed to simulate a stock market website such as yahoo finance. The app is made as a part of a university project. The app uses the flask library for python to work and run. Relevant data, such as stock data and news, is extracted from a local database. Features that could be improved, where it not for time constraints, would be a clear distinction between stocks you have "bought", and stocks you have put on your watchlist. Additionally, graphs that shows a stocks increase or decrease in value over time would be a great feature.

The project was made by Jan Alexander Peter Johannsen (pqs846) and Asger Rendsmark (jkw944).
The source code can be found at our Github repository.
